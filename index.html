Research Interest

My research interests are in the area of network security, particularly in security in cloud computing, monitoring network, and rule acces control and privacy. My current research is formal specification and verification of security. My goal is to develop and evaluate a distributed network infrastructure in the context of security. I am also interested in application of network security in education and specifically in tutoring systems. I am involved in a research project which is about using learning algorithm for pen test. My focus in this research project is on assessing the quality of results of algorithms in an attacker behavior.
